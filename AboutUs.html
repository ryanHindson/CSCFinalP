About Full Court Performance




Full Court Performance provides a social platform and offers services to players, coaches, and basketball enthusiasts. Whether you want to improve your personal skills, learn more about the game or be up to date with the latest news regarding all things basketball, Full Court Performance has got you covered.

We look to offer service to players from a multitude of countries, leagues, and divisions. Our online tutorials offer skills development varying across many skill levels, from beginner to programs targeting atheletes at the pro/pro-am level.

The founders of FCP; Ivan Cheng, Ryan Hindson, Luke Rushton and Kennedy Talarico have worked effortlessly to ensure that you as users are provided with the most recent, professional and legitimate knowledge. That's why we're always looking for feedback from you so we can constantly improve our platform and keep our reputation as the internet's largest basketball repository.
